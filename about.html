Our Story
A production company built on the conviction that African stories deserve the world's attention.

Founded in Johannesburg in 1996, The Bomb Shelter Productions emerged at the dawn of South Africa's democracy ‚Äî a company born of a moment, and shaped by everything that followed. From the very beginning, our work has been rooted in a belief that storytelling is not entertainment alone: it is testimony, history, and the connective tissue of a nation in the making.

Under the creative leadership of Angus Gibson, the company has produced some of the most watched, most awarded and most culturally significant television dramas, feature films, documentaries and commercial work ever made on the African continent. From the raw social realism of Yizo-Yizo to the epic grandeur of Shaka iLembe, our catalogue spans nearly every genre and every register of the human experience.

We work with the finest writers, directors, cinematographers and crews South Africa has to offer. We collaborate with international partners who share our standards. And we pursue projects that matter ‚Äî stories that illuminate, challenge and endure long after the credits roll.

The Bomb Shelter is not just a production company. It is an institution ‚Äî an archive of South African life, rendered in moving image, that will outlast all of us.
